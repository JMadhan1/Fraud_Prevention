Prevention Tips
  • Never invest in "guaranteed" returns
  • Verify advisor credentials with SEBI
  • Be wary of high-pressure tactics
  • Research investment opportunities thoroughly
  • Don't share personal financial information
  • Trust your instincts - if it seems too good to be true, it probably is
Common Red Flags
  • Promises of guaranteed high returns
  • Pressure to "act now" or "limited time offers"
  • Unregistered investment advisors
  • Requests for upfront fees or deposits
  • Difficulty withdrawing invested money
  • Secretive or complex investment strategies
